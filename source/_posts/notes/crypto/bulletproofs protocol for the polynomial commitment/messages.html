@vbuterin in our paper Section 6.1 (page 25 Fig 4) we give a succinct protocol for proving knowledge of the preimage of any homomorphism h:Z^n -> G. I personally find this to be the simplest exposition of the “Bulletproofs” polynomial commitment scheme, which is captured as a special case. We also simplify the analysis and show it is a proof not just an argument (no computational assumptions required for extraction).
