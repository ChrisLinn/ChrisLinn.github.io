Daira Hopwood
Note that many of the files in that curvesearch repo are experiments that didn't work out. The ones that worked are amicable.sage (used for generating the Tweedle and Pasta cycles), halfpairing.sage (as the name suggests, for half-pairing BN cycles), and bls12halogen.sage and kss18halogen.sage (for generating "inverted lollipop" cycles; see my ZK Study Club talk on YouTube)
09:47
None of these cycle constructions will work with Curve25519. There's a cycle involving secp256k1, but it's not highly 2-adic so is not ideal for proof systems
09:48
Anyway, I think the Pasta curves are likely to become popular curves in future
